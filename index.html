How Language Practice Works

💬 Chat with the AI Tutor

  • Type messages in the target language to practice
  • The AI responds in the target language with explanations
  • Each word has a tooltip with its translation

🔍 Word Exploration

  • Hover over any word to see its translation
  • Click on a word for detailed grammar explanation
  • Use 📚 Dictionary button for definitions
  • Use 🔗 Examples button for usage examples

📝 Text Selection

  • Select text in AI messages with your mouse
  • A popup appears with 📚 Dictionary and 🔗 Examples buttons
  • Perfect for looking up phrases or sentences you don't understand
  • Works in AI messages only (not in your own messages)

✏️ Grammar Correction

  • The AI highlights errors in your messages
  • Hover over red-underlined words to see corrections
  • Click to see detailed explanation of the mistake

🌐 Translation Help

  • Use 🔁 button to translate your input
  • Click 🌐 on AI messages for full translation

⚙️ Customize

  • Click ⚙ to change learning/explanation languages
  • Your preferences are saved automatically
